Introduction

The Avengers are a superhero team from Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). They are known for their collaborative efforts to defend Earth and the galaxy from various threats. The team's history spans comic books, films, and other media, with different iterations and members throughout the years.
